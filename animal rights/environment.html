How Plant Based Diets Influence the Environment



Overall for environmental effets, swithcing to plant based diets help lower water and land use, decrease pollution and greenhouse gases, slow down deforestation and destruction of habitats, and help reduce destruction of topsoil.
Half of all the land that is habitatble on our Earth is utilized for agricultural purposes. About 30% of this is used for plant production, while the other 70% of land is used for animal production.

29% of freshwater in the world is used for animal production and farming. The productions of one pound of beef uses 1800 gallons of water. Whereas to produce one pound of soybean, which is used to make plant protein like tofu, utilizes only 216 gallons of water.
Animal production and agriculture attrivutes for almost 15% of all global greenhouse gas emissions. This is a higher percentage than all global transportation combined.
