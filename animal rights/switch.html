Making the switch



We understand how hard change can be. We proudly offer different resources in order to make the transition to a cruelty-free lifestyle as easy and smooth as possible. We advocate for animals because we care, and we hope we can inspire change in others.

Each meal without meat, dairy or any other animal by-products helps. We do not expect those interested in change to make drastic changes overnight, but rather to actively make compassionate choices each day leading to less animals suffering cruel condtions.

Plant-based diets have many benefits in addition to cutting down on animal cruelty in the agriculture industry. These benefits include but are not limited to environmental, personal health, and moral benefits.
