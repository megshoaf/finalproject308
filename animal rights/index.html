About Us

We are a non-profit organization focusing on animal welfare and ending animal cruelty through the dairy, meat, and fashion industries. We began our journey in 2019, with founder of our group Megan Shoaf. Megan has been a dedicated animal rights activist and studied conditions of factories. She has also participated in a plant-based diet and vegan lifestyle since 2015.

We have provided links to some other animal rights based webpages in hopes of inspiring research and change. Thank you for your support!
